A few bits about me...

Hi there, my name is Oliver. Throughout my life I have had a vested interest in video games. For as long as I can remember, I've been in love with the medium with a passion and interest that spills even outside of the game experiences themselves. From reading eagerly about the latest upcoming developments and releases in magazines, to trawling through whatever breadcrumbs I could find about the development of my favourite titles, I've always been hooked.

It's this interest that has given me the lifelong goal of working within the video game industry as a Game Producer/Designer. With this in mind, I want to gain a bed of knowledge within coding and production and use that to attain experience and progress within the industry. I studied Computer Science throughout my GCSEs, learning Python and PHP, and into my A-Levels. With the latter ending prematurely, due to circumstances outside of my control, and not knowing where to pursue my interest further, the ambition had to take a backseat to realistic then-local options as I entered the world of work.

However, through the pandemic, I allowed my interest to be reinvigorated as I started once again consuming content more focused on game development - this time with more of a slant towards design philosophies. This helped to inspire me and think more critically about why mechanics and design had been implemented in particular ways.

My main inspiration is Masahiro Sakurai, best known for being the creator of Kirby and Super Smash Bros. He also has his own YouTube channel where he discusses all manner of subjects relating to games, their design, and development. Some of my favourite video game genres are JRPGs, Puzzle Games, and Professional Wrestling Games; with some of my favourite titles in each genre being Persona 5 Royal, Portal 2, and Virtual Pro Wrestling 2: Ōdō Keishō (Royal Road Succession).

Hobbies & Interests

I am currently learning Japanese using online resources and textbooks. I was supposed to visit Japan in 2020, but my travel plans had to be abandoned due to COVID-19.

I love Sudoku and other puzzles/problem-solving challenges.

I spend a lot of my time conceptualising video games I would like to create in the future. This even spills into games I'm currently playing as I'll find myself making notes based on concepts I enjoy and would like to expand on or take inspiration from.

When inspiration strikes me, I also like to draw. Usually my inspiration takes the form of professional wrestling, as it is such a colourful and dynamic medium.
